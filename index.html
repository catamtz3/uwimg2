What is a Complexion Cupid?

A “Complexion Cupid” is a virtual ally designed to revolutionize the often daunting color-matching process. Many know that even professional makeup artists undergo extensive training to comprehend the intricacies of people's skin tones, undertones, winter/summer shades, and specific skin concerns. Unfortunately, this process can become even more overwhelming for individuals with colorblindness or those residing in communities with limited resources, such as access to physical stores.

The Complexion Cupid seeks to demystify and overall simplify the color-matching process. Unlike methods that require a physical store visit, which can be unattainable for various communities, including those in rural areas, lacking local stores with color-matching services or facing barriers like inaccessible transportation, Complexion Cupid fully operates online!

Our innovative program invites all individuals, including makeup novices, to upload an image and let the Complexion Cupid work its magic. The program will provide valuable insights into your skin tone through our skin analysis. With this information, the goal is to recommend potential foundation matches available online. The ultimate aim is to ensure a seamless and inclusive experience for all, regardless of familiarity with makeup.
